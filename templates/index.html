In electronics, a wafer is a thin slice of semiconductor material (like silicon) used to manufacture microchips and solar cells. During the fabrication process, wafers undergo steps such as doping, etching, ion implantation, and photolithography to build integrated circuits.

This project focuses on detecting sensor faults during wafer manufacturing using Machine Learning models. It helps identify faulty wafers early, improving product quality and reducing manufacturing losses.
